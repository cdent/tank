TANK

For Your Tiddlers

An indexed store and API for your bits of stuff on the web, hypertexted for your pleasure.

We call them tiddlers, keep them in tanks and work with them in compositions.

Do with your tiddlers what you will: Make a wiki, keep your private or public notes, build single page applications, manage data over the API.

Please note, for the time being Tank is in an open beta, free for anyone to try. At some point in the future it will become a subscription service to ensure its continued support and maintenance. Stay in touch to learn more.
